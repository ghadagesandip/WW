Content Delivery Network

What is a Content Delivery Network?

A Content Delivery Network (CDN) ensures your customers and visitors spend less time waiting on content – reducing lost opportunities and lost sales. CDNs store a copy of your web content on local servers, which can be defined by city, country and every continent worldwide – so your visitors get your content from the closest possible location.

Web Werks allows you to use as many of these locations as you like, to accelerate all kinds of web content such as static and dynamic pages, images, audio, video and file downloads.

How can I customize?

Building your custom Content Delivery Network is easy, select your estimated monthly usage, choose the locations that matter to you, tell us how much you’re willing to spend, define what sort of quality you need, and we’ll tell you how long it’ll last. Or if you’re not sure just launch our global package, and refine it as you analyze the results.
